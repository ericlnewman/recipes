Lasgna


Ingredients


  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1.24 pounds (567 grams) bulk italian sausage
  • 0.75 pounds (340 grams) ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 43 ounces (1.27 liters) of crushed tomatoes, 1 can is 28 ounces, the other is 15 ounces
  • 12 ounces (354.9 ml) of tomato paste, 2 cans
  • 2/3 cups (157.72 ml) water
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons (29.57 ml to 44.3 ml) sugar
  • 3 tablespoons (44.3 ml) fresh parsley
  • 2 teaspoons (9.85 ml) dried basil
  • 0.75 teaspoons (3.7 ml) fennel seed
  • 0.75 teaspoons (3.7 ml) salt
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 15 ounces (442.6 ml) ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups (946.35 ml) part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 0.75 cups (177.44 ml) grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Follow noodles' direcitons according to the package; drain. Meanwhile, in a dutch oven, cook sausage, beef and onion over medium
    heat 8-19 minutes or until meat is no longer pink, breaking up meat into crumbles. Add garlic; cook 1 minute. Drain.
  2. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar, 3 tablespoons parsley, basil, fennel, 0.5 teaspoons salt and pepper; bring to a boil.
    Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a small bow, mix egg, ricotta cheese and remaining 0.25 cup parsely and 0.25 teaspoon salt.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190.56 degrees C). Spread 2 cups meat sauce into an ungreased 13x9 inch (330.2x228.6 mm)
    baking dish. Layer with 3 noodles, and a third of the ricotta mixture. Sprinkle with 1 cup mozzarella cheese and 2 tablespoons
    parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers twice. Top with remaining meat sauce and cheese (dish will be full).
  5. Bake, covered, 25 minutes. Bake, uncovered, 25 minutes, or until bubbling. Let stand for 15 minutes prior to serving.
